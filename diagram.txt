Flow Notes:
- Entrance → Check-In: verify appointment and identity basics; hand out rules sheet.
- Check-In → Lockers: candidates secure personal items before testing.
- Lockers → ID Verification Desk: formal ID check, seating assignment, and system setup review.
- ID Verification Desk → Orientation Briefing: short walk-through of procedures, prohibited behaviors, and restroom policy.
- Orientation → Test Room: proctor escorts candidate to assigned seat; camera/AV team confirms feed and mic.
- During exam: Cam/AV monitor station watches feeds; proctor floor walk for disruptions.
- Post-exam: Quiet exit path to avoid disturbing active testers; optional debrief or incident notes.
